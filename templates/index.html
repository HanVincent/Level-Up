"Submit your article, and click the sentence.
We will examine your grammar level."
Unlike many approaches to GEC, this approach does NOT require annotated training data and mainly depends on a monolingual language model. The program works by iteratively comparing certain words in a text against alternative candidates and applying a correction if one of these candidates is more probable than the original word. These correction candidates are variously generated by a spell checker, morphological inflection database, or are otherwise defined manually. Currently, this system only corrects these sentences.
